What is Alkami?

What makes us different?

We're a team of entrepreneurs who are passionate, well-rounded developers. But we are more than code monkeys.

Our team brings an unparalleled perspective to every project. With backgrounds in government, project management, finance, and business strategy; and career experiences ranging from law enforcement officers to athletes.

We use this experience to create user friendly, elegant, responsive web applications for any industry. Every app we build is responsive across all screen sizes, conforms with conventional best practices, and is well documented and well factored. We believe making an app is like building a custom high performance vehicle minus the expensive maintenance costs.

Getting Started

Let's chat about your project

The best way to get started is a quick chat, either over coffee or by phone or skype. We'll sit down, get to know you and what you are trying to do, give you an idea about us and see if we are a good match. We choose our projects carefully, seeking out not only people with great ideas who want to solve problems, but people who are passionate about what they do, because that is who we are.

Our Skills

The ones that pay the bills

Development

Our people are multilingual problem solvers. here are some of the technologies we're proficient in: Ruby On Rails, Test Driven Development, Agile methodologies, REST, API's, PostgreSQL, Redis, Web-Scraping, Oauth, Heroku, Digital Ocean, Amazon Web Services, JavaScript, AngularJS, ReactJS, NodeJS, ExpressJS, WebSockets, HTML5 Canvas, New Relic, Skylight, Chrome Dev-Tools, WordPress, Static Blog Generators.

Design

Want to hear our dirty little secret? We love design! Users usually only see the front-end of an app/web-site and we want that first impression to be pleasant. At Alkami, we know usability is a crucial part of a users experience. We take great pride in designing cool simple interfaces. Our experts understand user experience and build intuitive interfaces. We're proficient in SCSS, Bohemian Sketch, Mobile First Design, and UX/UI principles.

Communication and Partnership

Communication is a key component of any project. Our clients are very involved with our teams, in fact we are one of the most transparent teams you will have the chance to work with. You know exactly where your project stands and where it's going. You always have access to us via email, telephone, and access to the project chat channel for daily insights into what is happening with your project.
